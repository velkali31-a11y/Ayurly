About Us

MAKa Stevia Innovations

MAKa Stevia Innovations is a leading stevia manufacturing and R&D industry in Madurai. It was inaugurated in 2014 by Muthu Krishnan. J. We specialize in the research and development, cultivation, and manufacturing of natural sweetener "Stevia".

Why Non-Nutrition?

Modern diets contain hidden sugars that contribute to obesity. MAKa Stevia Innovations provides a solution with its zero-calorie Stevia products, helping reduce unnecessary calorie intake.

Our Vision

Our goal is to reduce excess calorie intake in children and adults with our 100% natural Stevia extracts. We market directly to consumers and businesses, focusing on long-term health benefits beyond diabetes.

Our Social Responsibility

We are committed to promoting sustainable agriculture by reducing water consumption compared to sugarcane. Our products come in various forms, including powder, leaves, liquid, and sweets, providing a healthy sugar alternative.
